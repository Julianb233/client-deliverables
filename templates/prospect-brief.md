## 4. Recommended Discovery Questions
*Open-ended, probing, educational questions for Rob to ask during the initial meeting. These questions must avoid prompting specific transactions and focus solely on understanding the prospect's goals and goals-based planning needs.*

1. **Vesting & Cash Flow:** "How do your upcoming equity vesting dates fit into your broader short-term cash flow and savings goals?"
2. **Concentration Risk:** "What are your thoughts on having a significant portion of your net worth or compensation tied to your employer's stock?"
3. **Tax Planning Cadence:** "When you look at your RSU vestings or option exercises, how do you currently coordinate that with tax planning?"
4. **Goal Prioritization:** "What other financial goals—such as retirement planning, buying a home, or education funding—are most important to you as we evaluate your overall financial picture?"
5. **Holding Strategy:** "Do you typically hold your vested shares or look to diversify them into other goals? What has been your decision-making process there?"
