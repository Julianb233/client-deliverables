# Better Together App Icon Research - Round 2

Date: 2026-07-12

## Primary guidance

- [Apple Human Interface Guidelines: App icons](https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/app-icons/)
- [Apple Human Interface Guidelines: Motion](https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/motion)

Apple's current guidance favors one simple, unique core idea, a minimal number of shapes, centered primary content, square unmasked source layers, consistent features across default/dark/tinted appearances, and enough visual weight to survive system scaling. Layered source art is preferred because the system can add depth and environmental response.

## Current App Store benchmarks

- [Headspace](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/headspace-sleep-meditation/id493145008): one oversized circular character with three facial strokes. Recognition comes from silhouette and personality, not detail.
- [Paired](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/paired-couples-relationship/id1469609343): two large organic forms create one relationship symbol. The two-part construction maps directly to partnership and supports convergence motion.
- [Locket](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/locket-widget/id1600525061): one recessed heart on a high-contrast field. The icon owns one object and one material effect.
- [Finch](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/finch-self-care-pet/id1528595748): a tightly cropped mascot face fills the canvas. It demonstrates the value of committing to a single recognizable subject.
- [Agape](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/agap%C3%A9-feel-close-when-apart/id1507907556): a one-color geometric seal uses thick repeated curves. It shows that a seal can work on the App Store when the geometry is bold and the silhouette remains singular.
- [Calm](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/calm/id571800810): a highly distinctive wordmark on a simple color field. Better Together should not copy the text-led approach because the name is too long for small icon contexts.

These products are references for structural discipline only. No artwork, geometry, color palette, or trademark is being copied.

## Round-two design rules

1. Garden Seal is the parent system.
2. Every concept must be a true 1:1 square and readable at 20-29 px.
3. Use one seal, one living center, and no more than four dominant shapes.
4. Preserve two-part meaning: two arcs, two stems, or two leaves become one living mark.
5. Keep the Better Together palette: `#FAF8F5`, `#E5533A`, `#113B36`, `#D99A3D`.
6. Avoid text, pots, literal people, infinity marks, decorative scenery, thin hairlines, and pre-rounded icon masks.
7. Prepare production as three layers: background, seal, living center.
8. Motion must explain the relationship: separate -> approach -> connect -> grow -> settle.
9. The same core geometry must survive default, dark, monochrome, and tinted appearances.
10. Approval selects a system, not a raw generated bitmap. The winning concept still receives exact vector geometry and Icon Composer layers.
