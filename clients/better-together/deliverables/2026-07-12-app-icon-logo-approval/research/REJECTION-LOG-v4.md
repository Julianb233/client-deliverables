# Round 4 Curation Log

Round 4 began with five user-requested territories: a bold BT monogram, BT with bonsai growth, a heart built into a bonsai, an open two-part heart, and a wordmark-first BT signature.

Thirteen icon renders were reviewed to curate the five icon sources on the approval board. Five matching full-name lockups were then generated, for 18 reviewed renders and 10 final review assets.

## Corrections

1. **BT Living Ligature** - Rejected the first blocky, bullseye-like construction. The selected version has a clearer paper B, coral T, and single living node.
2. **Rooted BT** - Rejected stray root shapes that read like feet. The selected version keeps the bonsai story in the three-leaf crown growing directly from the T.
3. **Heartwood Bonsai** - Rejected a cloud-like canopy and an extra secondary heart. The selected version makes the large heart through the two trunks and keeps one shared canopy.
4. **Open Heart Pair** - Rejected excess near-colors and a flame-like center. The selected version uses two independent heart halves and one simple saffron seed.
5. **BT Editorial Signature** - Rejected a floating crossbar and a weak letter junction. The selected version has a cleaner serif BT, one coral bridge, and one saffron period.

## Review Gates

- All 10 final files are 1024 by 1024 PNG assets and passed the technical image validator.
- All five lockups spell **Better Together** exactly.
- Three icons passed every automated small-size and palette signal.
- Heartwood Bonsai and BT Editorial Signature passed human legibility review after the automated palette counter treated antialiasing and soft raster texture as extra colors. Both require vector simplification after approval.
- No production or TestFlight icon has been changed in this exploration round.
