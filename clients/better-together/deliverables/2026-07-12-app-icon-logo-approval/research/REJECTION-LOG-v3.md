# Better Together Round 3 rejection log

Date: 2026-07-12

Eleven Nano Banana renders were generated through the versioned prompt pipeline. Automated gates checked dimensions, file integrity, palette discipline, centering, visual mass, complexity, and 20 px contrast. Human review then rejected seven renders that passed some or all automated checks but failed meaning or originality.

| Direction | Decision | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Paired Canopy, first render | Reject | Added its own rounded app-icon mask and read as an oversized broccoli/tree form. |
| Paired Canopy, correction | Reject | Removed the mask but became two generic trees with a detached seed. |
| Rooted Duo, first render | Reject | Resolved into a goalpost or tuning-fork silhouette. |
| Rooted Duo, correction | Reject | Resolved into musical notes rather than a relationship or growth mark. |
| Promise Seed | Reject | The paired halves formed a heart-like silhouette. |
| Promise Seed, precision edit | Reject | The heart was removed, but the mark became a generic flame. |
| Shared Crown | Reject | Added a rounded mask and read as an umbrella held by hands. |

Confluence Sprout was explored in three renders. Its clearest version was retained in the generation record but not promoted because the connected symbol lacked enough visual mass at 20 px. The approval board contains only the four directions that passed both measured and human review.

No third-party icon artwork was provided to Nano Banana. App Store icons were downloaded to an ignored local cache for structural measurement only and are not republished in this board.
